# Mergington High School Activities

A friendly website where students can explore and sign up for extracurricular activities at Mergington High School.

## What Can You Do on This Website?

### For Students
- **Browse all activities** — See every club, team, and workshop available at the school.
- **Search for activities** — Type a keyword to quickly find what you're looking for.
- **Filter activities** — Narrow down activities by:
  - **Category** — Sports, Arts, Academic, Community, or Technology
  - **Day** — Find activities on a specific day of the week (Monday through Sunday)
  - **Time** — Before school (6–8 AM), after school (3–6 PM), or on the weekend. Activities outside these time slots will only appear when "All Times" is selected.
  - **Difficulty** — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
- **View activity details** — See the schedule, difficulty level, and how many spots are still available.

### For Teachers
- **Log in** — Use the Login button in the top right corner to sign in with your teacher account.
- **Register students** — Once logged in, you can sign a student up for an activity using their school email address.
- **Unregister students** — You can also remove a student from an activity if needed.
